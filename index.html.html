Who We Are

Our Origin & Vision

Founded in North Bengal, Tistaguri Audiostory represents a bold new chapter in Bengali audio storytelling. We are strategically expanding our footprint across West Bengal, connecting districts through the universal language of compelling narratives.

We collaborate with elite writers and professional voice artists spanning multiple districts. Our content transcends regional boundaries, speaking to the shared experiences and emotions of all Bengali listeners.

Our long-term vision is to become West Bengal's dominant audio brand — combining measurable audience growth with sustainable financial models, ensuring predictable scaling and market leadership.
